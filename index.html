The Amoija Vision:

Started in 2014 by two brothers from Stellenosch, Amoija Events was born from out of love for sport and respect for the great outdoors. The vision has always been clear- create world-class endurance events, inspired by the mighty mountains of the Western Cape.


Our Events:

Events held on exclusive routes, available only to Amoija entrants.

Amoija also organises 3rd party events. Highly skilled and experienced guns for hire who help bring your vision onto the trails.


Our Mission:

Amoija aims to become give athletes a testing endurance challenge, backed by an unrivaled event experience. We are striving to become a leader in creating experiences, not merely adding more races to the calendar.
